Research & Publications

📄 Conference Paper 📅 April 9, 2026
Data-Driven Platform for Student Placement Prediction and Personalized Career Development using Machine Learning

The contemporary employment landscape requires graduates to have not merely strong academic credentials but also industry-aligned competencies. This research proposes an intelligent, data-driven web-based platform that predicts placement probability while delivering personalized career development strategies. The system constructs comprehensive student profiles using academic indicators — secondary and higher secondary scores, CGPA, backlog history — alongside a dynamic online assessment evaluating aptitude, technical proficiency, and communication skills. An ATS-based resume evaluation component analyzes resumes for keyword relevance, structural coherence, and formatting quality. Supervised ML algorithms including Logistic Regression, SVM, Decision Trees, and Random Forests estimate placement probability, with the optimal model achieving approximately 88% accuracy. Results are presented through an interactive dashboard that benchmarks performance and identifies improvement areas, while the platform also provides actionable recommendations including skill pathways, certification suggestions, project ideas, and structured 30/60/90-day preparation roadmaps.
